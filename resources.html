Macomb Student Resources

Think of your student experience like the writing process. We often treat resources—like counseling, advising, or food pantries—as "Later Order Concerns" that we will get to eventually. But actually, these are your High Order Concerns (HOCs).

If the big stuff isn't working (like your mental health, your food security, or your degree plan), the "small stuff" (like tonight's reading) becomes impossible. Macomb has a department to help you handle the HOCs so you can focus on the rest. Here are the direct links.
