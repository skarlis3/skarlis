Final Portfolio

ENGL 1181 — Independent Study

Your final portfolio is your chance to show what you've learned and how you've grown as a writer and student this semester. It has two parts:

  1. Genre Analysis Essay (Final Draft) — A final revision of your genre analysis essay
  2. Final Reflection — A multimodal reflection on your growth, strengths, and learning this semester

Both parts are submitted separately, but the portfolio is graded as a whole.

The Final Reflection is a multimodal project. This means it should use visual design intentionally — not just text dumped into slides. Before you start building it, you'll read about visual rhetoric and multimodal texts so you have a foundation for making design choices. See the Final Reflection page for details and readings.

Grade Weights

  • Genre Analysis Final Draft: 20% of your course grade
  • Final Reflection: 15% of your course grade

Due Dates

  • Genre Analysis Final Draft: Due Sunday, May 3
  • Final Reflection: Due Sunday, May 10
