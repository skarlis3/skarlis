  • TED Paragraphs: Building Strong Body Paragraphs — Framework for structuring body paragraphs with topic sentence, evidence, and discussion (assigned Week 6)
  • MLA Formatting & Citations — Basics of MLA format, in-text citations, and Works Cited (assigned Week 6)
