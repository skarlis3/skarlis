Example Essay Organization

Here is the full essay structure in one view. Each row is a section of your paper. This is a flexible framework — all of these sections are required, but some may be longer than shown here (e.g., more convention paragraphs, a two-paragraph intro, or a longer genre function section).

Intro

Introduce the professional context and the genre you will analyze. State that your essay focuses on IRAC briefs.

Genre Description

Describe the genre. Explain its purpose (why it exists) and audience (who it's for).

Convention 1

Name one common feature. Show examples from your samples. Explain why it matters.

Convention 2

Name a second common feature. Show examples. Explain why it matters.

Convention 3

Name a third common feature. Show examples. Explain why it matters.

Genre Function

How does this genre support legal practice? What does it reveal about the field's values?

Conclusion

Summarize your findings. Restate how this genre functions in professional practice.
