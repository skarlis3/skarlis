Project 2: Exhibit Day & Visitor Report

Arrival & Setup

On Exhibit Day, please do the following when you arrive in class::

  1. Setup: Find a spot and set up your exhibit.
  2. Safety: Make sure all bags/stuff are put away and not going to be a trip hazard for students walking around.
  3. Identification: Make sure your name is clearly visible on the project. If not, get a post-it note from me to add it. This ensures I know whose project it is when I take photos for grading records.
