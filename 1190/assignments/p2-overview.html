Project 2: Museum Exhibit from the Year 2050

Overview

For Project 2, you are going to take the research and conclusions you drew from Project 1 to create a multimodal "museum exhibit" display. You will include artifacts from your Project 1 and new artifacts from the near future, up until 2050 (you get to make stuff up here), to show how AI evolved in your field.

The Premise

The year is 2050. You are curating an exhibit on how AI changed your field.

  • Take research/conclusions from Project 1.
  • Create a museum exhibit for the year 2050.
  • Include artifacts from Project 1 (the past) and new "made up" artifacts from the near future (2027–2050).

Step 1: Decision Time

First, you need to decide what direction AI in your field went. You can choose from the following options:

  • The Golden Age (A Utopian Future): The most optimistic research was correct. AI fixed problems and helped the field flourish. Everything is better than ever.
  • System Failure (A Dystopian Future): The warnings were ignored and some of the worst predictions came true. The field became a mess.

For either of these paths, you get to decide what happened. Just make sure your ideas are rooted in the conversations and research that is happening now.
