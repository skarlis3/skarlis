Weekly Writing 2

Major or Career Focus

Requirements

Length: At least 200 words
Genre: Short and informal essay (write in your own voice!)
Assignment AI Policy: Do not use GenAI for this assignment.

For more information about these assignments, see the Weekly Writing Overview.

Overview

Build on what you wrote for Weekly Writing 1 to clarify which major or career you want to focus on this semester and what you know or wonder about it.

If you need more information on a job/career path while writing, the Occupational Outlook Handbook is a helpful starting point.

Instructions

  1. State your focus: At the top of your document, name the major and/or career you plan to focus on this semester.
  2. Respond in a short and informal essay (in paragraph form; don’t list the prompts):
    • What do you already know about this career/major? What interests you about it?
    • What concerns do you have about it as a potential path?
    • What do you know (if anything) about how technology is or isn’t used in this field (software/apps, equipment, etc.)?

Note: Use the prompts to guide a thoughtful response—do not copy/paste them and answer one by one.

Grading
