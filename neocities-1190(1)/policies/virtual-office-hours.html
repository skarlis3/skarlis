How to Meet

For first-time Teams users:

  • From your phone, download Teams from the Play/App store and then log in with your Macomb email and password, or
  • From a computer, go to the Microsoft Teams site and then log in with your Macomb email and password.
  1. During virtual office hours, go to Microsoft Teams and ensure you are logged in with your Macomb ID.
  2. Once the app is open, click on Chat ① in the left navigation.
  3. Click on Search ② and search for my name.
  1. Select my name from the drop-down list to open up a chat.
  2. Enter a message in the text area at the bottom of the chat and click the send icon ③.

Note

If you are using the mobile app, the Chat button is at the bottom of the screen when you first log in. Once you click on Chat, Search appears at the top of the screen.

Quick Tips for Virtual Office Hours

  • Make sure you’re logged into Teams using your Macomb student email.
  • If you can’t find me in Search, double-check spelling and confirm you’re in the correct Macomb directory.
  • Send a message first — I’ll start a video call if needed.
  • If your audio/video isn’t working, the chat feature can be used for full support.
