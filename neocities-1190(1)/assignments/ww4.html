Weekly Writing 4

News About AI in Your Career/Field

Requirements

Length: No minimum
Genre: Question & Answer; uploaded PDFs (3 minimum)
Assignment AI Policy: Do not use GenAI for this assignment.

Overview

For Weekly Writing 4, we will continue to explore AI in your potential field/career. This week we will look at new articles to get a sense of what is happening in the field. We will also start to work on evaluating sources for this assignment.

Instructions

  1. First, find at least three news articles on AI in your potential field/career.
    • Note: A news article should come from a newspaper (online or otherwise), not a company website or blog. Consider using AP news, news.google.com, or selecting “News” from a Google or Bing search to find the articles.
  2. Read the articles and then save them as PDFs (Similar to WW3, you will be submitting them with this assignment).
  3. In a Word doc, answer the following for each news source:
    Note: Just list these answers for each of the articles (no need to write in essay/paragraph format).
    • Name of article
    • Name of publication (i.e. name of newspaper)
    • Date published
    • Source assessment:
      • Is this article a news story or an opinion article? How can you tell?
      • Does the article seem biased? Based on opinion? Does it seem backed with research? How can you tell?
      • How does this article relate to the other articles you saved and read? (i.e. does it talk about different uses of AI? Does it take a similar or difference stance? etc.)
      • How does this article relate to the conversations you read/watched online for Weekly Writing 3?
  4. Finally, upload the news article and document to Canvas.
