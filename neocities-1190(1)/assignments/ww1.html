Weekly Writing 1

College Major & Possible Career Path

Requirements

Length: At least 150 words
Genre: Short and informal essay (write in your own voice!)
Purpose: Brainstorm a potential major or career to focus on this semester.
Assignment AI Policy: Do not use GenAI for this assignment.

For more information about Weekly Writing assignments, see the Weekly Writing Overview.

Overview

This section of ENGL 1190 will focus on college majors/career paths and the impact and trends of artificial intelligence in those fields. For this first Weekly Writing, you’ll write a short essay exploring your major and/or possible career paths.

Instructions

Choose one of the following options for your short essay:

  1. Option 1: You have a declared major or plan
    What is your major? Why is it interesting to you or why did you choose it? What do you plan to do with this major (what careers are you considering)?
  2. Option 2: You’re undecided or exploring
    What majors and/or careers are you considering? What about them interests you? Which one(s) would you be interested in focusing on this semester as a starting place for your writing and research?

Reminder: Always check the requirements above for the minimum word count each week. Don’t list the prompts and answer them one-by-one—use them to guide a coherent paragraph-based response.
